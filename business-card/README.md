# DawnriseCamp Business Card

This folder contains an editable business card design for an outdoor products foreign trade manager.

- Front: `dawnrisecamp-card-front.svg` uses the reference layout: left brand panel, right contact panel, bottom product bar.
- Back: `dawnrisecamp-card-back.svg`
- Logo asset: `assets/company-logo.png`

Print setup:

- Artboard: 96 x 60 mm
- Trim size: 90 x 54 mm
- Bleed: 3 mm on each side
- Inner safe guide: 3 mm inside trim

Replace these placeholders before printing:

- `YOUR NAME`
- `+86 000 0000 0000`
- `sales@dawnrisecamp.com`
- `www.dawnrisecamp.com`
- `Your company address, China`
- QR / WeChat placeholder

Recommended paper: 350 gsm matte art card or soft-touch laminated card. Spot UV can be added to the logo or orange wave for a more premium outdoor trade-show feel.
